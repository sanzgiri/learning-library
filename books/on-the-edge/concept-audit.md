# On the Edge Concept Audit

Checklist against the book's own glossary, "How to Speak Riverian" (250+ entries, read in full from the epub) plus the book's real chapter text (also read in full for the relevant sections, not summarized from reviews).

**Methodology note, deviating from the standard format on purpose:** Taleb's Incerto glossary (~34 entries) was small enough to enumerate every term individually. This glossary is an order of magnitude larger, and the large majority of its entries are vocabulary — poker hand names, betting mechanics, crypto/AI technical jargon — not distinct ideas, which `MISSION.md` explicitly puts out of scope. Enumerating all 250+ individually would bury the handful of real findings in noise, which cuts against this skill's own pruning discipline. So: distinct ideas get individual status below; pure vocabulary is grouped into named clusters with a count and representative examples, not enumerated term-by-term.

## Vocabulary clusters — bulk deferred, not individually enumerated

- **Poker hand/game mechanics** (~50 terms): 3-Bet, Aces, Ante, Blinds, Button, Flop, Fold, Hold'em, Nuts, Outs, Range, River, Set, Showdown, Straight, Suited, Tilt, Whale, etc. Vocabulary, not ideas.
- **Sports betting mechanics** (~10 terms): point spread, moneyline, vig, parlay, total, cover, etc.
- **Finance mechanics** (~20 terms): arbitrage, bid-ask spread, Black-Scholes, hedge fund, index fund, IRR, leverage, market-maker, ROI, etc.
- **Crypto vocabulary** (~15 terms): blockchain, NFT, HODL, rug pull, shitcoin, Ethereum, etc.
- **AI technical vocabulary** (~15 terms, distinct from the AI *risk argument*, which is covered in 0012): AGI, attention, compute, GPU, LLM, neural net, RLHF, token, transformer, Turing test, etc.
- **Named people, events, and proper nouns**: DonBest, WSOP, the Traitorous Eight, specific poker players and VCs quoted as sources, etc.

## Net result

13 lessons covering the load-bearing ideas. 6 genuinely open ("planned") for a future pass: Bayesian reasoning, the prisoner's dilemma, the nuclear-deterrence cluster, the motte-and-bailey fallacy, wisdom of crowds, and adverse selection. ~230 remaining glossary entries are vocabulary, deliberately out of scope per `MISSION.md`.
